Gwinnett County's Oldest and Finest​​ Tattooing Establishment.


Open since 1997, Good Clean Fun has been serving the tattoo community of Gwinnett County and the surrounding ares for over 20 years.


At Good Clean Fun, all of our equipment is Autoclave sterilized/single use. You can bring your own design, or have us create a one of a kind custom design for you. We also have lots of awesome designs for you to chose from.


Walk-ins are always welcome, or you can schedule an appointment in person (a CASH deposit is required to schedule an appointment/have a custom tattoo designed).
